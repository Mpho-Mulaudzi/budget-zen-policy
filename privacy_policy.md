# Privacy Policy — Budget Zen

_Last updated: October 2025_

Thank you for using **Budget Zen**.  
This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information provided or collected while you use it.

---

## 1. About This App

**Budget Zen** is a lightweight Android/iOS calculator built with Flutter.  
It supports both dark modes and may display banner ads provided by Google AdMob.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

The app **does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII)**.

However, the **Google Mobile Ads SDK** (AdMob) may automatically collect certain non‑personal and analytics information required for displaying ads, such as:

- Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID)
- Approximate location (for regional ad relevance)
- App usage data (e.g. number of sessions, crash statistics)
- IP address and device type

All of this processing follows Google’s [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 3. Advertising and Cookies

This app uses Google AdMob to serve ads.  
AdMob may use cookies or similar technologies to display personalized or non‑personalized ads.  
You can manage your advertising preferences within your Google Account or mobile‑device settings.

---

## 4. Consent for EU/EEA Users

If you are located in the **European Economic Area (EEA)** or UK, you will be prompted for consent regarding personalized ads.  
You may withdraw or change your consent anytime through your device’s “Ads Settings.”

---

## 5. Children’s Privacy

Smart Minimal Calculator does **not** target or knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your country).  
If you believe a child has provided us information by mistake, please contact us so we can delete it.

---

Changes will be reflected in this document with a new “last updated” date.
